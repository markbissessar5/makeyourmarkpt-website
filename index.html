The Person Behind the Programme

Meet Your
Coach

Founder & Head Coach
Mark
Founder, Make Your Mark Performance Coaching

Mark started Make Your Mark because he was tired of watching people get handed the same generic programme regardless of their goals, their schedule, or where they were actually starting from. He built this business around a simpler idea: training should be designed for the person doing it, not the other way around.

Based in Sydney, Mark works with everyone from first-timers rebuilding a habit to competitive athletes chasing a season, to lifters who already know their way around a barbell and want real structure. Every programme starts with a conversation, not a template — your goals, your training history, and your week get mapped before a single session is written.

On the floor, Mark's approach is direct and hands-on: technique first, consistency over intensity, and progress you can actually measure. Off the floor, that means real check-ins and programming that adjusts as your life does — not a plan that falls apart the first time work gets busy.

  • Personalised programming across recomposition, performance and strength
  • Hands-on coaching, in-person and online across Sydney
  • Ongoing check-ins and programme adjustments, not a set-and-forget plan
